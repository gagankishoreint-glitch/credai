# User Manual

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Modern Web Browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection (for initial load)

### Accessing the System
The system is deployed and accessible at: [https://credit-evaluation-system.vercel.app](https://credit-evaluation-system.vercel.app)

---

## Features Guide

### 1. Loan Application
**Purpose**: Submit a new loan request for instant AI evaluation.
**Steps**:
1. Navigate to "Home".
2. Scroll to the "Application Form".
3. Fill in business details (Revenue, Cash Flow, Loan Amount).
4. Click "Check Eligibility".
5. View instant decision (Approved/Review/Rejected) along with your AI Risk Score.

### 2. Compare Loans
**Purpose**: Compare two different loan offers side-by-side.
**Steps**:
1. Go to "Compare" tab.
2. Enter details for Loan A and Loan B (Interest Rate, Tenure, Fees).
3. Use the **Priority Sliders** to indicate what matters to you (Low EMI vs. Total Interest).
4. System highlights the "Best Match" based on your preferences.

### 3. EMI Calculator
**Purpose**: Understand the true monthly cost of a loan.
**Steps**:
1. Go to "Calculator" tab.
2. Enter Loan Amount, Interest Rate, and Tenure.
3. Add hidden costs like Processing Fees and Insurance.
4. View the "Real Monthly Burden" and "Affordability Check".

### 4. Document Analyzer
**Purpose**: Get a credit strength check from your bank statement.
**Steps**:
1. Go to "Analyzer" tab.
2. Drag and drop your bank statement (PDF/Image).
3. System analyzes transaction patterns.
4. View your "Credit Strength Score" and improvement tips.

### 5. Market Benchmarks
**Purpose**: See how your financial ask compares to the market.
**Steps**:
1. Go to "Benchmarks" tab.
2. View current average interest rates by region.
3. Check market trends for popular loan products.

## Troubleshooting

- **Issue**: "Prediction Failed" error.
  - **Solution**: Ensure the backend server is running. If using the demo site, refresh the page.
  
- **Issue**: Document upload stuck.
  - **Solution**: Ensure file is under 10MB and in PDF/JPG format.
