Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI-driven credit underwriting platform.

How fast is the evaluation?

Our AI processes applications in milliseconds. While traditional underwriting can take days or weeks, CredAi delivers comprehensive risk assessments instantly, allowing you to make real-time lending decisions.

Is the data secure?

Yes. We use banking-grade encryption (AES-256) for all data in transit and at rest. Our platform is SOC 2 Type II compliant, ensuring your applicant data is handled with the highest security standards.

How accurate is the model?

Our Nonlinear Dynamic Models consistently outperform traditional logistic regression scorecards. In a recent study of Korean credit data, our model achieved an AUC of 0.958 compared to 0.906 for traditional methods.

Can it handle "thin-file" applicants?

Absolutely. We specialize in evaluating underserved borrowers by analyzing alternative data sources—such as rent payments, utility bills, and bank transactions—to build a complete financial profile even without a deep credit history.
