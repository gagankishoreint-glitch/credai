Dual Analysis for SMBs

For small business lending, evaluating the business entity alone is often insufficient. Our platform simultaneously analyzes the business credit profile and the personal guarantor's history, merging them into a single, comprehensive risk report.

Alternative Data Streams

We integrate diverse data sources to illuminate "hidden" creditworthiness:

  • Banking transaction velocity
  • Utility and repayment history
  • Rent payment consistency (via open banking APIs)
  • Cash flow trends and seasonality
