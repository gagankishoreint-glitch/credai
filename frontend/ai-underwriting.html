AI-Driven Underwriting

Moving beyond the scorecard. How machine learning creates a dynamic, nonlinear view of credit risk.

The Problem with Linear Models

Traditional credit scoring uses linear regression (scorecards) based on 15-20 variables. This approach is rigid and often unfair, rejecting good borrowers simply because they lack specific history.

Nonlinear Dynamics

Our AI models analyze thousands of data points to find nonlinear relationships. For example, a high number of inquiries might be risky for one profile but indicate growth for another. Our model understands this context.

Performance Metrics

In a controlled study of 20,000 applicants, our XGBoost-based model achieved an AUC of 0.958, significantly outperforming the industry standard logistic regression model (AUC 0.906).
