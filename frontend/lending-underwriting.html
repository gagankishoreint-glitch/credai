Reducing Bias

Traditional "box-checking" underwriting often discriminates against younger demographics or recent immigrants. Our models are rigorously tested for disparate impact, ensuring decisions are based on ability to repay, not demographics.

Serving the Underserved

By leveraging alternative data like rent payments and cash-flow analytics, we help lenders safely approve creditworthy borrowers who have been invisible to the traditional bureaus.
