Scoring Factors

Structured Credit Evaluation

Our model analyzes a synthetic training dataset of 2,000–7,000 records to learn the complex relationships between key financial indicators:

  • • Annual Revenue: Revenue in ₹ or $
  • • Years in Operation: Business stability
  • • Cashflow: Monthly avg. cash flow
  • • Credit Score: Numeric (300–900)
  • • Debt-to-Income: Financial health ratio
  • • Existing Loans: Active liabilities
  • • Collateral Value: Asset value
  • • Repayment History: Good/Avg/Poor
  • • Default Flag: Target (0=No, 1=Yes)
  • • Business Type: Mfg/Trading/Services
